How to Compress PDFs Effectively

A short guide to reducing PDF sizes while balancing quality.

Start by identifying images in the PDF. Re-encoding images using JPEG compression often yields the largest savings. Also consider removing embedded fonts, metadata and unused objects. For scanned documents, OCR and re-saving as a lower DPI can help. When using client-side tools like ToolMetric, ensure you test outputs on a sample page to confirm acceptable quality. Always keep a backup of the original file before compressing.
