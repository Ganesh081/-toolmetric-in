About Compress PDF to 200KB

This tool compresses PDF files entirely in your browser. Files never leave your device. It creates a new PDF structure and copies pages into it, which often reduces file size. For best results with image-heavy PDFs, optimize images before compression.

Step-by-step guide

  1. Upload your PDF.
  2. Click "Compress" to process.
  3. Download your compressed PDF.

FAQ

Will my file be uploaded?

No. Processing happens locally in your browser.

What if the file isn't smaller?

Try optimizing images before compression.

Is there a file size limit?

Depends on your device memory.
