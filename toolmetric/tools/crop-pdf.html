About Crop PDF

Crop PDF lets you remove margins or whitespace by setting margins in points (1 point = 1/72 inch). The operation modifies the media box of each page to the requested area.

Step-by-step guide

  1. Upload your PDF.
  2. Enter margins as top,right,bottom,left.
  3. Click "Crop" and download.

FAQ
