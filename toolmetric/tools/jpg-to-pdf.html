About JPG to PDF

This utility embeds an uploaded image into a PDF page sized to the image dimensions. It's ideal for converting photos or scanned images into shareable PDF files.

Step-by-step guide

  1. Choose a JPG or PNG image.
  2. Click "Convert" to embed it into a PDF.
  3. Download the resulting PDF file.
