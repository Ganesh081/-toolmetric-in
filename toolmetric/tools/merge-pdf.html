About Merge PDF

Merge PDF is designed to concatenate multiple PDFs into a single file preserving page order. It's useful when combining reports, receipts, or scanned pages into one document. The merging process happens entirely on your device to protect privacy.

Step-by-step guide

  1. Upload the PDFs in the order you want them merged.
  2. Click "Merge" to combine them.
  3. Download the merged PDF file.
