About PDF to Word

This conversion uses PDF.js to extract textual content from each page then packs it into a simple .doc file. Styling and complex layout elements like multi-column text, tables, or embedded images may not be preserved. The tool is best for text-heavy documents where content extraction is the primary goal. Because the process runs locally in your browser, no document is uploaded, ensuring privacy. The output is a basic Word-compatible file that can be further edited in Microsoft Word or other word processors.

Step-by-step guide

  1. Upload your PDF.
  2. Click "Convert" to extract text.
  3. Save the .doc result and refine formatting in Word.
