About Remove Blank Pages

Use this tool to clean up scanned documents or exported PDFs that contain empty sheets. The detection is heuristic and analyzes page content streams to decide which pages are likely blank. Running locally ensures documents never leave your device.

Step-by-step guide

  1. Upload your PDF.
  2. Click "Remove Blank Pages".
  3. Download the cleaned PDF.
