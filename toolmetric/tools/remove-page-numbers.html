About Remove Page Numbers

This tool attempts to detect common footer page numbers and redact them by overlaying a white rectangle. This approach works best on documents where page numbers are clearly separated from page content. Because the method is not perfect, inspect the output to ensure important content wasn't obscured.

Step-by-step guide

  1. Upload your PDF.
  2. Click "Remove Numbers" to apply redaction overlays.
  3. Download and verify the result.

FAQ

Will this recover original text?

No — it overlays content with white rectangles; original text beneath cannot be restored.
