About Reorder Pages

Reorder the pages in your PDF by defining a comma-separated list of page numbers in the desired sequence. Pages are 1-based.

Step-by-step guide

  1. Upload a PDF.
  2. Enter the new page order as numbers separated by commas.
  3. Click "Reorder" and download the rearranged file.
