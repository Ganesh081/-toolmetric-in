About Rotate PDF

Rotate PDF changes the orientation of every page in the document. Useful for scanned documents or when pages are upside down. The operation is performed locally so your data stays private.

Step-by-step guide

  1. Upload a PDF.
  2. Select rotation angle.
  3. Click "Rotate" and download.
