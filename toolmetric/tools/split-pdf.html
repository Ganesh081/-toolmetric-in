About Split PDF

Split PDF allows you to extract exact pages or page ranges to create a new PDF. This is useful for sharing only the relevant portion of a document or creating smaller PDFs from a larger source. The operation runs completely in-browser, so your data remains private.

Step-by-step guide

  1. Upload a PDF.
  2. Enter pages or ranges like 1-3,5.
  3. Click "Split" and download the new PDF.

FAQ

Can I split multiple ranges?

Yes — separate ranges with commas.

Are pages 1-based?

Yes — use 1 for the first page.

Do files leave my device?

No.

Supported browsers?

Modern browsers.
