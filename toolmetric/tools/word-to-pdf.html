About Word to PDF

This tool reads plain text from simple Word or text files and lays it out into a PDF using client-side PDF generation. Complex .docx features such as tables, images, and advanced styles may not be preserved in this lightweight conversion. For more involved conversions, use dedicated desktop tools. The benefit of ToolMetric's approach is privacy and speed — conversion is performed locally and is free to use.

Step-by-step guide

  1. Upload your .doc or .txt file.
  2. Click "Convert" to generate a PDF.
  3. Download and open the PDF.
